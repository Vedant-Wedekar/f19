Celebrate Ganpati with a unique and eco-friendly twist with our handcrafted Chocolate Ganpati Murti. Made from high-quality chocolate, this idol combines tradition with innovation, offering a sweet way to honor Lord Ganpati. The idol is 100% edible and designed for a respectful and eco-conscious visarjan, dissolving seamlessly in milk or water. After the visarjan, the dissolved chocolate can be used to prepare delicious prasad desserts, blending devotion with sustainability.
