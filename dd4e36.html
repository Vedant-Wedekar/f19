    Our Mission

    The mission behind Bappa Aradhana is to honor and celebrate the spiritual essence of Ganpati Bappa while reconnecting the modern generation, especially busy professionals and Gen-Z, to Maharashtrian culture. Through our platform, we provide easy access to beautifully crafted Ganpati murtis, eco-friendly decoration services, and essential puja items, making it simpler to embrace and uphold tradition in today’s fast-paced world. Beyond facilitating festive needs, Bappa Aradhana strives to inspire a sense of devotion and cultural pride by making Ganpati worship accessible, engaging, and sustainable. We aim to bring communities together in reverence and celebration, while fostering a connection with the rich cultural heritage that shapes our identity.

    Our Story

    We are Team Bappa Aradhana, Vedant and Anushka, both 2nd-year students at GHRAU University. The idea started as part of our mini-project, with Anushka suggesting a website dedicated to Lord Ganpati. After taking a couple of days to refine the concept, Vedant came up with an innovative idea: an e-commerce platform to provide Ganpati murtis and decoration services. Through thoughtful planning and creative design, we finalized Bappa Aradhana as our project. Our goal was clear: to address the growing disconnect we noticed among busy professionals, younger generations, and Maharashtrian customs, particularly during Ganpati celebrations. With minimal resources and a shared passion for our culture, we built this website from the ground up. Bappa Aradhana is our way of bringing the festive spirit closer, making it easier for everyone to celebrate with traditional puja essentials, murtis, and decorations—all at their fingertips.
